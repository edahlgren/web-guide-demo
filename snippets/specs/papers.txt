  papers

  Research papers and reports


  Quick reference
  ===============

   View the configuration

     $ cat /demo/demo.yml 
     
   Check this section

     $ demo configure papers --check
     

  Summary
  =======

   The papers section contains information about research papers that describe
   functionality of the demo and that are packaged in the demo for easy access.


  Requirements
  ============

                                                         
   papers: . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . .  Published papers that describe the demo
                                                          + Must be an array
                                                         
     - path: . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . .  Path to the published paper
                                                          + Must be a string
                                                          + Can't be missing or empty
                                                          + Must be an absolute file path
                                                         
       year_published: . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . .  Year the paper was published
                                                          + Must be a number
                                                          + Can't be missing or empty
                                                         
       authors: . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . List of authors of the paper
                                                          + Must be an array
                                                          + Can't be missing or empty
                                                         
         - . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . .  Author full name
                                                          + Must be a string
                                                          + Can't be missing or empty
                                                         
       keywords: . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . .  List of main concepts that this paper 
                                                        describes
                                                          + Must be an array
                                                          + Can't be missing or empty
                                                         
         - . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . .  Main concept as a phrase or keyword
                                                          + Must be a string
                                                          + Can't be missing or empty
                                                         
   

  Example
  =======

     papers:
       - path: /docs/papers/RussianMissiles.pdf
         year_published: 1986
         authors:
           - Mikhail Gorbachev
           - Konstantin Chernenko
         keywords:
           - Russian Nuclear Missile Design
     
       - path: /docs/papers/KoreanMissiles.pdf
         year_published: 2012
         authors:
           - Kim Jong Un
         keywords:
           - Korean Nuclear Missile Design
     

  Best practices
  ==============

   + List up to 10 authors (first 10)

   + Avoid packaging papers that are not published in an open access journal

   + If a paper isn't freely available, contact the author for help
